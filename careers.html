Careers in VFX

Not sure where you fit in the VFX world? Each step of the pipeline has its own team of artists who contribute different skills. Explore the careers below to see what each role does and what tools they use.

FX Artist Concept Artist Storyboard Artist 3D Modeller Texture Artist Animator Compositor

FX ARTIST

What Does an FX Artist Do?

FX Artists create explosions, fire, magic spells, weather effects, and other simulations for films, games, and animation. They blend technical skills with artistic creativity to make things move and react realistically or stylistically on screen.

Tools Used:

  • Houdini
  • Maya
  • Unreal Engine
  • Blender

How to Get Started:

Start with tutorials in Houdini or Niagara, learn basic simulation logic, and practise small effects like sparks, smoke, or magic auras.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Junior FX Artist
  • Technical Director – FX
  • Simulation Artist

CONCEPT ARTIST

What Does a Concept Artist Do?

Concept Artists create the early visual ideas that guide the entire project. They sketch characters, environments, props, and moods to define the style before anything is modeled or animated. Their work sets the creative tone and helps the team understand what the final world should look like.

Tools Used:

  • Photoshop
  • Procreate
  • Krita
  • Sketchbook / Traditional Media

How to Get Started:

Learn drawing fundamentals, study colour and lighting, and practise creating moodboards, styleframes, and quick visual ideas. Try redesigning your favourite characters or scenes to build your portfolio.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Junior Concept Artist
  • Character Concept Artist
  • Environment Concept Artist

STORYBOARD ARTIST

What Does a Storyboard Artist Do?

Storyboard Artists turn scripts into shot-by-shot visuals. They map out camera angles, character poses, timing, and action so the team understands how each sequence should play out.

Tools Used:

  • Photoshop
  • Procreate
  • Krita

How to Get Started:

Learn film language, practise drawing quick poses, and break down movies into storyboard frames. Re-storyboard scenes to improve timing and composition.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Storyboard Artist
  • Animatic Artist

3D MODELLER

What Does a 3D Modeler Do?

Modelers create detailed 3D characters, props, and environments. They transform concept art into fully realized digital forms with clean, animatable geometry.

Tools Used:

  • Maya
  • Blender
  • ZBrush

How to Get Started:

Practise modeling simple objects, study topology, and recreate props or scenes from your favourite films and games.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Character Modeler
  • Environment Modeler

TEXTURE ARTIST

What Does a Texture Artist Do?

Texture Artists paint the surfaces of 3D models. They create materials such as skin, metal, fabric, rust, or stone to make objects look realistic and believable.

Tools Used:

  • Substance Painter
  • Substance Designer
  • Photoshop

How to Get Started:

Practise UV unwrapping, study real materials, and recreate textures from photos. Build material studies to improve your portfolio.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Texture Artist
  • Look Development Artist
  • Material Artist

ANIMATOR

What Does an Animator Do?

Animators bring characters and objects to life through movement. They create performance, timing, and emotion using poses, motion paths, and acting principles.

Tools Used:

  • Maya
  • Blender
  • Motion Capture Tools

How to Get Started:

Study the 12 principles of animation, practise simple motions, and recreate clips frame-by-frame to learn timing and weight.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Character Animator
  • Motion Designer

COMPOSITOR

What Does a Compositor Do?

Compositors combine rendered 3D layers, effects, and live footage into a final, seamless image. They adjust color, depth, lighting, and details to make everything feel unified and believable.

Tools Used:

  • Nuke
  • After Effects
  • Fusion

How to Get Started:

Practise greenscreen keying, colour correction, and combining render passes. Study film shots and try recreating them from layers.

Jobs You Can Get:

  • Junior Compositor
  • Prep Artist
  • Digital Compositor
