Resources

A list of helpful tutorials and communities to guide your learning in VFX. Explore the links below to develop your skills, learn new techniques, build your portfolio, and meet other artists.

TUTORIALS COMMUNITIES

Communities

If you’re looking to get involved in the VFX world, these communities are great places to start. They offer spaces where artists share their work, exchange feedback, post tutorials, and talk about the industry.

ArtStation

A great platform for sharing your work, exploring portfolios from other artists, and finding inspiration. Many industry professionals post here, making it a good place to see high-level work and track current trends in VFX and digital art.
Check it out here.

CGBoost Community

A friendly community focused on Blender and general 3D art, great for beginners looking to improve their skills. They offer challenges, feedback, detailed tutorials, and courses taught in a very easy-to-understand way.
Here's a link to the site

The Rookies

A supportive community with members ranging from complete beginners to experienced professionals. They offer feedback, educational resources, mentorship opportunities, and even free classes. It’s an excellent space for learning, improving, and connecting with others.
Sign up to become a member here.

Tutorials

Here’s a list of some of our favourite tutorials – all free! These are great starting points for learning the basics and building your skills.

Houdini

  • SideFX – Houdini Foundations: Free official lessons introducing nodes, interface, and procedural workflows.
  • Entagma: Amazing free tutorials for procedural FX, simulations, and node logic.
  • CGMatter: Fast, practical beginner tutorials on getting results quickly in Houdini.

Download it here.

Maya

  • Academic Phoenix Plus: Clear, beginner-friendly modelling and UV tutorials.
  • Mike Hermes: Short, focused lessons covering essential Maya tools.
  • Arvid Schneider: Free tutorials on lighting and rendering with Arnold + Maya.

Download it here.

Blender

  • BlenderGuru: Gold-standard beginner series; very clear and friendly.
  • Filmstorm: Great for cinematic lighting and short-film style tutorials in Blender.
  • Grant Abbitt: Quick beginner tutorials covering modelling, sculpting, and fundamentals.

Download it here.

Unreal Engine

  • Unreal Online Learning: Free official beginner courses.
  • William Faucher: Fantastic lighting + cinematic tutorials.
  • Ben Cloward: Clear explanations of shader and material systems.

Download it here.

Photoshop

  • PiXimperfect: Excellent tutorials on selections, cleanup, and retouching.
  • Ctrl+Paint: Free lessons on digital painting fundamentals.
  • Marc Brunet: Free beginner lessons for concept-art workflow.

Download it here.

Procreate

  • Art with Flo: Step-by-step beginner drawing lessons.
  • James Julier: Great for learning landscape painting.
  • Bardot Brush: Good intro to brushes and textures.

Learn more here.

Krita

  • Krita Foundation Tutorials: Official beginner guides.
