Resources

A list of helpful tutorials and communities to guide your learning in VFX. Explore the links below to develop your skills, learn new techniques, build your portfolio, and meet other artists.

Communities

If you’re looking to get involved in the VFX world, these communities are great places to start. They offer spaces where artists share their work, exchange feedback, post tutorials, and talk about the industry.

ArtStation

A great platform for sharing your work, exploring portfolios from other artists, and finding inspiration. Many industry professionals post here, making it a good place to see high-level work and track current trends in VFX and digital art.
[Check it out here.]

CGBoost Community

A friendly community focused on Blender and general 3D art, great for beginners looking to improve their skills. They offer challenges, feedback, detailed tutorials, and courses taught in a very easy-to-understand way.
[Here's a link to the site]

The Rookies

A supportive community with members ranging from complete beginners to experienced professionals. They offer feedback, educational resources, mentorship opportunities, and even free classes. It’s an excellent space for learning, improving, and connecting with others.
